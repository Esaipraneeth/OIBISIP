SACHIN RAMESH TENDULKAR

Here's a time line of Sachin-Tendulkar:

  • 1973-Mumbai,Maharastra
  • 1989-Sachin at the age 16 years 205 days became India's youngest Test cricketer in international by making his debut against Pakistan in Karachi in November 1989
  • 1996-at the age of 23 Sachin was made captain of team India
  • 1998- Sachin was chosen for the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award. The highest award given to an Indian athlete, for his outstanding performance in between 1997-98
  • 2012- Sachin scored his record-setting 100th international century which included both 51 Test centuries and 49 ODI centuries. He retired from ODI cricket later that year
  • 2012-Sachin became a member of the Rajya Sabha the upper chamber of the Indian parliament and becomes the first active athlete to join that body, he was nominated to the post and his term ended in 2018.
  • 2014-Sachin became the first sportsman to receive India's highest civilian honour The Bharat Ratna.
  • 2019-Tendulkar was inducted into the International Cricket Council Hall of Fame in 2019.
